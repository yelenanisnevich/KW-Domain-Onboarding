License Transfer - MiPLUS
Transfer your Michigan real estate license to KW Domain

Before we can get you up and running, you'll need to transfer your Michigan real estate license to KW Domain through MiPLUS. We've made it easy with step-by-step instructions below.

Just passed your exam? It may take up to a week for your testing info to show up in MiPLUS. In a hurry? Call LARA directly: 517-241-0199
You'll need our broker license number
KW Domain Broker License #
6505391898
Step-by-step: How to transfer in MiPLUS
  1. Go to MiPLUS and log in.
  2. Once logged in, select the Licenses tab above the welcome message.
  3. Find your license in the list and click Modification next to your license number.
  4. Select "Transfer or Employing Broker Notification" and click Continue Application.
  5. Scroll to New Employing Broker Information and enter our 10-digit Broker number: 6505391898. The company name and address will auto-fill.
  6. Review your information and click Continue Application to submit.
  7. Save your confirmation record number - then send Agent Services a quick message to confirm it went through!
Once submitted, KW Domain receives a pending-approval notification. No further action needed - just let us know it's done!
